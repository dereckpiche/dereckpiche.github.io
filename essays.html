Causality | September 29 2024


The fundamental root of human understanding is causality. If you understand something, its because you model a causal relation somewhere.

(Correlation is not causation but is caused by it. That two variables are correlated simply means that they are, somewhere in the causal graph, descendants of at least one common ancestor variable.)

But if causality is to be treated as a phenomenon, we could only understand it through our understanding apparatus, which relies on causality... Here there is no mirror with which the eye can stare at itself.
