Where Are we Going? (in developpement)

Civilization is developping faster than ever. Then is it not obvious that we should ask if its going in the right direction?

The Data

Let us first analyze the recent curves on metrics which aim to be proxies for human wellness.

GDP per Capita

GDP measures the total production of goods and services within a country. The simplest way to get this metric is to substract the value of what you created to the cost you paid for getting the ressources to create it. It is a measure of the total productivity of a nation. Obviously, this metric will often grow as you add more people so you want to divide by population size. This metric has been on the rise since 1990: Conclusion: most countries are getting more productive.
